- Sales performance
- Profitability trends
- Discount impact
- Product-level analysis

💡 Goal: Improve business decisions using visual analytics

- Applied consistent color strategy for storytelling
- Used scatter plot for Discount vs Profit analysis
- Optimized dashboard for clean UI

- Generate business insights

📊 Dashboard Insights

- Government segment contributes 67% of sales
- High discounts reduce profit significantly
- Paseo is top product (~$33M contribution)
- Sales vary between 5M–12M monthly
- Low discount = highest profit
- Small Business = 24.52%, Midmarket = 3.91%

🧠 Key Insights
- Revenue is highly concentrated in Government segment
- Discounts directly reduce profitability
- One product (Paseo) drives major revenue
- Profit drops when discount increases
- Strong seasonal variation in sales
- Business depends on few key products
  
📈 Analytics Approach
 
- Descriptive → $119M overview
- Diagnostic → Discount vs Profit
- Predictive → Sales trend
- Prescriptive → Optimize pricing strategy

📌 Final Conclusion

- Strong revenue base: $119M
- Government = main driver
- Paseo dominates sales
- Discounts hurt profitability
- Seasonal trend is clear
- Pricing optimization is required
